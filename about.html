About Us

Team BigFootIsReal

At Team BigFootIsReal, we are a dedicated group of data enthusiasts, developers, and machine learning enthusiasts, united by a common goal: to make a positive impact on healthcare through innovation. Our journey begins at a hackathon, where we set out on a mission to revolutionize the field of ophthalmology. With a shared passion for technology and a deep sense of responsibility toward global healthcare, we've come together to develop a cutting-edge machine learning model for the early detection of diabetic retinopathy.

Accessible Healthcare

FWe are committed to making our solution accessible to all, with a strong emphasis on cost-effective, user-friendly tools that can be deployed across diverse healthcare settings.

Continuous Improvement

Our team thrives on innovation and believes in the power of continuous improvement. We are dedicated to refining and enhancing our model to stay at the forefront of diabetic retinopathy detection.

Precision and Accuracy

Our machine learning model harnesses the power of advanced algorithms to achieve unparalleled accuracy in detecting diabetic retinopathy, ensuring early intervention and improved patient outcomes.

Global Impact

Beyond the hackathon, we aspire to create a global impact by addressing a critical healthcare issue and contributing to the well-being of diabetic patients worldwide.
